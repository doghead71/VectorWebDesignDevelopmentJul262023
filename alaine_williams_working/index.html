About Me

I'm Alaine Williams. Hello. As an IT student currently employed in the finance sector.

The physical side of information technology has always drawn me in. My father enjoyed creating personal computers from scratch, and a close friend of mine insisted on driving me to job sites so I could observe a data network technician in action. It seemed certain that much earlier than now, I would be swiveling network connections or carefully handling hard drives. But I struggled to finish my accounting courses for a significant portion of my adult life. I consciously and sincerely decided to pursue my goal of working in information technology in 2018. I decided to pursue my studies at Vector Technology Institute because I thought the environment there was ideal for both my personality and my skill level, or lack thereof. You may trust me when I say that the choice was the greatest and that I am enjoying every second—or should I say, every module—of it.
