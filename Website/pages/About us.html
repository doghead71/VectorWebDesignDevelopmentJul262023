About Us

RJR Events, Decor & Rentals is the brainchild company of founder Kerri-ann McFaralane. The company initials RJR was so named after the first letters of her 3 children. The company is so named because of the founders love for planning events and having multiskill achievements is various skill areas, such as fashion, interior design and decor, floral arrangement and a host more.

RJR Events, Decor & Rentals was founded June 2019 and located in Portmore, St, Catherine, Jamaica.

In our own words
